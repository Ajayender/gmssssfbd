Vision

Our Vision is to be recognized as a Centre of Excellence in the field of education by meeting the challenges of the present & the future. Our aim through “quality education to all” is to unlock the students’ potential, in still determination & self confidence through constant effort & encouragement and to grow rapidly by establishing a strong tradition of high quality, reliability and free education to students from all sections of the society.

From The Principal's Desk

Our mission is ‘True to education’. Education is viewed with a holistic approach. Our endeavor is to :
1. Help students develop to their fullest potential, physically, mentally, emotionally, psychologically and spiritually.
2. Promote the development of basic skills, positive attitude, analytical thinking and inquiry based learning.
3. Imbibe in them the quality of appreciation and respect for cultural diversity.

-Satender Kr. Sorout
